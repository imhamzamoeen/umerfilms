# Story 3.1: About Page with Video Introduction

## Status
Ready for QA

## Story
**As a** visitor,
**I want** to watch a video introduction from Umer and read about his background,
**so that** I can understand his personality and decide if he's the right fit for my project.

## Acceptance Criteria

1. About page is accessible at `/about` route
2. Page displays heading ("About" or "Meet Umer")
3. Video introduction is prominently displayed using VideoPlayer component
4. Video does NOT autoplay (user-initiated playback)
5. Video has poster image showing Umer (thumbnail before play)
6. Text bio section appears below/beside video (2-3 paragraphs about Umer)
7. Bio content includes: who he is, his passion, what makes him unique
8. Optional: skills/specialties list (videography, photography, editing, etc.)
9. Optional: equipment/gear section (camera, software - can be deferred)
10. Page is responsive with video and text stacking on mobile
11. Page metadata (title, description) is set for SEO
12. CTA at bottom links to Work With Me page

```typescript
// src/app/about/page.tsx

import { Metadata } from 'next';
import Link from 'next/link';
import { PageWrapper } from '@/components/layout';
import { VideoPlayer } from '@/components/video';

export const metadata: Metadata = {
  title: 'About - UmerFilms',
  description: 'Meet Umer, a cinematic videographer passionate about crafting visual stories that capture authentic moments and compelling narratives.',
  openGraph: {
    title: 'About Umer - UmerFilms',
    description: 'Cinematic videographer and visual storyteller',
  },
};

export default function AboutPage() {
  return (
    <PageWrapper>
      <div className="py-16 md:py-20">
        <h1 className="text-4xl md:text-5xl font-bold mb-8 md:mb-12 text-center">
          Meet Umer
        </h1>

        {/* Video Introduction */}
        <div className="mb-12 md:mb-16">
          <VideoPlayer
            src="/videos/about/intro.mp4"
            poster="/videos/about/poster.jpg"
            autoplay={false}
            controls={true}
            className="w-full"
          />
        </div>

        {/* Bio Section */}
        <div className="max-w-3xl mx-auto mb-12 md:mb-16">
          <div className="space-y-6 text-lg md:text-xl text-gray-300 leading-relaxed">
            <p>
              I'm Umer, a cinematic videographer passionate about crafting visual stories
              that capture authentic moments and compelling narratives. With years of
              experience in commercial and creative videography, I bring your vision to
              life through thoughtful composition and powerful storytelling.
            </p>
            <p>
              My journey in filmmaking began with a fascination for how moving images can
              evoke emotion and communicate ideas beyond words. Whether it's a brand launch,
              a wedding celebration, or a creative passion project, I approach each film
              with the same dedication to excellence and artistic integrity.
            </p>
            <p>
              What sets my work apart is the combination of technical expertise and genuine
              human connection. I believe the best films happen when the camera disappears
              and real moments unfold naturally. Let's create something extraordinary together.
            </p>
          </div>
        </div>

        {/* Optional: Skills Section */}
        <div className="max-w-3xl mx-auto mb-12 md:mb-16">
          <h2 className="text-2xl md:text-3xl font-bold mb-6">Specialties</h2>
          <div className="grid grid-cols-2 md:grid-cols-3 gap-4">
            {['Videography', 'Cinematography', 'Editing', 'Color Grading', 'Storytelling', 'Direction'].map((skill) => (
              <div key={skill} className="bg-gray-800 rounded-lg px-4 py-3 text-center">
                {skill}
              </div>
            ))}
          </div>
        </div>

        {/* CTA */}
        <div className="text-center">
          <Link
            href="/contact"
            className="inline-flex items-center justify-center px-8 py-4 text-lg font-medium bg-amber-600 text-black hover:bg-amber-500 transition-colors rounded-lg"
          >
            Let's Work Together
          </Link>
        </div>
      </div>
    </PageWrapper>
  );
}
```
